 DCC Garch Model and VaR

we have 4 stocks apple, google, nike and mircosoft. The dataset is from 2009.12.14 to
2012.12.12. We estimate the 4 stocks as portfolio with Dcc Garch model. The value at
risk for 95% for this portfolio is around 11. From the backtesting we cannot reject null hypothese, which means our Dcc garch model is suitable.
